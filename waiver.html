What this space is

This is a place where raw unfiltered human emotion. It is not a safe space. You may encounter content that is intense, heavy, or emotionally distressing. That is intentional.

Assumption of risk

By entering, you accept that you will encounter content that is offensive, graphic, or emotionally hostile. This content exists because real human beings needed to release it. You are choosing to witness that.

The venting clause

Nothing posted here reflects real-world intent. All content is expression, not action. Anger is not a threat. Grief is not a diagnosis. What you read here is someone's darkest moment made safe by being said out loud. It is not who they are. It is not who you are.

Your protection

You take full responsibility for your emotional experience here. The owners of this site are not liable for any distress arising from your visit. Every submission is reviewed and stripped of all identifying information before publication. Names, places, relationships are all removed.

What happens to what you write

Your submission goes to a human reviewer. It will be read, anonymized, and if it fits this space, published. If it contains a real name, a specific address, or a credible threat, it will not be published.

⚠  This site is not a substitute for professional mental health support.
